DARPA Learning Introspective Control (LINC) Project

I am the Technical Lead for the DARPA LINC project at ARCL. Our motivation is that traditional control systems can't handle unexpected conditions or damage outside their original design, and fixing them often takes specialized expertise and significant time.

LINC aims to develop machine learning methods that allow physical systems, like vehicles, ships, drone swarms, and robots, to adapt in real time to unforeseen events. LINC continuously compares sensor data to a learned model, detects critical changes, updates control laws as needed, and communicates those updates to the operator, whether human or autonomous. This enables rapid recovery, keeps operators informed, and maintains trust and control, even in unpredictable situations.
